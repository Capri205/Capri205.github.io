About Me

I will let my LinkedIn profile do most of the professional talking. I started off my programming life in BASIC and assembly language on my TI99-4/A when I was a school kid. My first IT job proper was as a COBOL programmer. Acient stuff, I know. I've wandered around the Tech scene for many a year in a number of different capacities, but programming has always been an integral part of it to varying degrees. I have always kept my hand in personally with various projects and hobbies, at one point running, supporting, coding for and administering 23 game servers for my kids and their friends out of my garage! Whilst most of that has wound down I still write Java plugins for the remaining parts of that network. I am currently employed as an IT developer at a local digital print operation writing and maintaining various systems such as inventory, order-management and labelling systems.
